Privacy Policy

This application enables users to upload videos to their own YouTube channels using the YouTube Data API v3.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All actions are initiated and authorized by the user through Google's OAuth2 authentication flow.

The app only accesses the minimum required scopes to perform video uploads and does not retain any user credentials or content.
